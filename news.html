ONLINE CLASSES - DBTI Okhla


Everyone is aware of the fact that there is a sudden surge in the cases of COVID-19 in Delhi. Therefore, all are hereby informed that as per the direction from the concerned department, the conduct of all the classes of DBTI Okhla will be online from 12th to 30th April 2021 and so students are asked not to come to the institute until then. If there is any update on the extension of the online classes beyond 30th April, the matter will be communicated before 30th April 2021. The trainees are asked to cooperate with the teachers with regard to online classes so that these days are made use properly for the trainees’ benefit. We are all aware of the prevailing situation which is quite alarming due to the spread of Covid-19 in Delhi NCT and so take at most care and be safe.
